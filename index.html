Short bio:
I am a robotics researcher specializing in cable-driven parallel robots and aerial robotics. Since 2020, I have been a full professor at the LCFC laboratory at the University of Lorraine. My work focuses on autonomous systems, human-robot collaboration, and sustainable automation for industrial and agricultural applications.
