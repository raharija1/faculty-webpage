Control system projects

This page presents a selection of control systems projects.
How to structure your project?

  1. Modeling
    • State-space equations
    • Linearization if necessary
    • Assumptions and simplifications
  2. System analysis
    • Observability / controllability
    • Stability
    • Bode plots / time responses
  3. Controller or observer design
    • LQR, PID, pole placement, Kalman, Luenberger
    • Justification of the chosen gains
  4. Simulation (Matlab/Simulink or Python)
    • Time responses
    • Disturbances and noise
    • Conclusion and limitations
